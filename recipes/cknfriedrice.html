Chicken Fried Rice

Description

    Ingredients:
  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Frozen Mixed Veggies
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Seasonings
    Steps:
  1. Cut chicken into cubes
  2. Season the chicken
  3. Set pan to medium heat and cook chicken (2 batches)
  4. In same pan, cook frozen mixed vegetables
  5. Crack open eggs and whisk
  6. When veggies are cooked through, move to side of pan
  7. Pour eggs into pan and cook til scrambled
  8. Pour cooked rice into the pan
  9. Mixe everything up
  10. Add soy sauce and chili flakes
  11. Add chicken to pan
  12. And you're ready to serve!
